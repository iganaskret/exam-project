tarot collection

Tarots are a pack of playing cards that became very popular in the mid-15th century Europe. From the 18th century they began to be used for divination and occult purposes. Why tarot cards? I find tarots and occult matters very fascinating because I think they work more with human subconscious than with actual divination and future reading, and all my art is trying to find myself, so it matches perfectly what tarot reading does.
For the drawings I just made the classic tarots with my style.
