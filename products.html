temprance

Temperance is one of the most valuable virtues still today. The card shows a female angel pouring a liquid from a cup to another, patiently. She’s in the water with one foot, symbol of the distractions that can come through what you’re doing and that you have to ignore in order to reach your final goal (the crown at the end of the path in the back.
